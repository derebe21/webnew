# How to Create Your Google Business Profile for ITSEC Technology

To establish **ITSEC Technology.com** securely on Google Maps and Google Search, follow these steps to claim your free Google Business Profile.

## Step 1: Create the Profile
1. Go to **[Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/)** and click **Manage now**.
2. Sign in with your official business Google Account (e.g., `info@itsectechnology.com`).
3. Enter your business name exactly as: **ITSEC Technology PLC**.
4. Choose the primary category: **Computer Security Service** or **Telecommunications Equipment Supplier**.

## Step 2: Add Location & Contact Info
1. Select **Yes** when asked if you have a physical location customers can visit.
2. Enter your address exactly as it appears on your website:
   - **Street Address:** Kirkos Church
   - **City:** Addis Ababa
   - **Country:** Ethiopia
3. Enter your phone numbers: `+251-911-407-439` or `+251-955-190-019`.
4. Enter your website URL: `https://itsectechnology.com`

## Step 3: Verification
Google will ask you to verify your business. Since you are in Addis Ababa, this will likely be via **Phone Verification** (getting an SMS/Call) or **Video Verification** (showing your office/equipment).
- *Follow Google's instructions to complete this step.*

## Step 4: Optimize Your Profile
Once verified, optimize your profile to rank on the first page:
- **Add Your Logo:** Upload your official ITSEC Technology shield logo.
- **Add Photos:** Upload high-quality photos of your data center projects, fiber optic installations, and team in action (like the images you sent me earlier).
- **Add Services:** Explicitly list your services from your website:
  - Data Center Construction
  - Fiber Optic Installation
  - Physical Security Systems
  - SCADA Control Systems
  - Cybersecurity & Networks
